John Huether Photography
Portfolio

John is a central New York based freelance photographer that will be attending Ohio State University studying Communication. He has spent the last five years starting his own photography company and obtaining his Associates degree in New Media With Advanced Sciences.

For the past few years, John has shot equine events, weddings, portraits and covered public events. Some of these include the Tompkins County bicentennial and sports at Tompkins Cortland Community College. John believes that photography and videography are both an art form and a way to tell a unique story. He uses both digital and medium format film to convey these themes.
